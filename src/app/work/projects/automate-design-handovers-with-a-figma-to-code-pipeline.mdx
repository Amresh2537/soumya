Overview
As part of her internship at MARS Exploration Pvt. Ltd., Saumya Gupta actively contributed to various domains of Human Resource Management. The internship helped her gain practical insights into core HR processes, especially in the areas of recruitment, policy drafting, and employee lifecycle management.

Key Features
Job Posting & Recruitment

Created and posted job descriptions for internship roles.

Identified platforms for mass hiring and scheduled timeline-based job postings.

Conducted 9 telephonic interviews, shortlisting candidates and drafting professional rejection notes.

HR Policy & Documentation

Authored documents explaining HRM principles and drafted HR policies for a specific industry.

Created a knowledge piece on Attendance Management including biometric and proximetric systems.

Listed and analyzed multiple selection methods used by modern organizations.

Onboarding & Engagement

Documented structured induction & orientation steps for new hires.

Suggested effective methods to boost long-term employee engagement.

Professional Development & Admin Tasks

Updated and maintained a Google Sheet for internship tracking.

Optimized personal LinkedIn profile to reflect HR experiences.

Technologies Used
Google Sheets: For tracking and reporting internship progress.

Google Docs: For preparing HR documents, job descriptions, and reports.

Telephony: For conducting candidate interviews.

LinkedIn: For professional networking and employer branding research.

Challenges and Learnings
Crafting role-specific policies and tailoring interview questions for varied candidate profiles were challenging yet enriching. Saumya learned the significance of clear communication, structured onboarding, and empathetic engagement strategies that resonate with today’s workforce.

Outcome
This internship helped Saumya gain hands-on exposure to HR fundamentals. She strengthened her confidence in recruitment strategy, document creation, and onboarding systems—laying a solid foundation for her future HR career.
